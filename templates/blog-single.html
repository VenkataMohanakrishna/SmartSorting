How MobileNetV2 is Revolutionizing Crop Sorting

Published on Sept 14 | By AI Team

In recent years, the intersection of agriculture and artificial intelligence has grown tighter. Transfer learning plays a huge role here by drastically reducing the data and compute requirements needed to train robust vision models.

By leveraging MobileNetV2, an architecture designed for mobile and resource-constrained environments, this project aims to predict the health of fruits and vegetables in milliseconds, ensuring only the best produce makes it to market.
